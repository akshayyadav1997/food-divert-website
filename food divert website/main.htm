FOOD DIVERT

Food Divert food sharing website allows you to donate your left overs to the needy.It has multiple features including current donation status,food quantity and serving details as well as transportation service.This website has an user friendly interface and graphics.Learn More

  • vision
  • Mission

To implement the system to all local users who can book the food for their own need. Add search and suggestion capabilities based on the location of the user. Add support for different languages to allow widespread acceptance of the platform.

The main objective to build this system is basically to prevent wastage of food in large quantity and also to contribute in reduction of malnutrition and it helps ngo, orphanages,old age homes.etc to manage their food needs.

IRIS


IRIS is an inter-college cultural festival. It is held annually in the month of February/March. A galaxy of events is held ranging from cultural to literary, gaming, management and marketing.


SFIT ensures all-round development of the students of the college through the various inter-college and intra-college events the best of which is the cultural festival IRIS. IRIS is the annual college cultural festival normally held in the even semesters. It is organized by the student body of the college with enormous zeal and enthusiasm. It is a channel through which students inculcate and sharpen their hidden skills like organization, management, administration and so on. It is a platform on which students bloom into entrepreneurs. IRIS was first organized in 2002 and it has been a college trend since then on a grand scale. The main events being


Performing arts
• Literary events
• Fine arts
• Gaming
• Glow-Night
• Rock Show
• DJ Night

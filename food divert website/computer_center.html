Computer Center

The Institute has several networked computer laboratories for use in a wide range of subjects from all three engineering disciplines. Each lab is customized to some extent for the needs of particular disciplines, but is available to all Institute students when not reserved for classes.
Names of Labs:

  • Project Laboratory
  • Database Laboratory
  • Matlab Image Processing and Simulation Laboratory
  • Orcad Pspice Laboratory
  • Rational Rose Laboratory
  • AutoCAD Laboratory
  • Multimedia Laboratory
  • Mobile Computing Laboratory
  • Microprocessor Laboratory

The SFIT library also has networked computers with On-line Library provides access to several Internet based research databases. These databases can be accessed from off-campus through Internet. Each Lab is equipped with Printing facility. Each Class Room is equipped with LCD Projector and Laptop for teaching with visual presentation. Final year Students are strongly encouraged to have their own computers for routine work, in order to avoid end-of-semester bottlenecks in the labs.
Institution has 8 Mbps leased line internet connectivity accessible for 24 hours.
